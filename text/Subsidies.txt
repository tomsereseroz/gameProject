Subsidies and insurance essentially benefit the seller and unnaturally influence the competitive capitalist market
It is the worst of socialism and capitalism - the mandatory inclusion of socialism/communism with the profit-maximization of capitalism.
A subsidy is, in effect, just the government directly paying producers to produce a larger quantity of product. This artificially distorts the market such that the subsidized products have an advantage in a free competitive market.
Insurance is similar in this way. Mandatory insurance forces people to pay for services that they may not need, in the same way that a subsidy would influence a consumer to buy a product they otherwise wouldn't if it was priced accurately.
